Doughnuts

Description

I have not yet tried this recipe. Will try to remember to do so when I try it. I want to try in the air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons melted shortning
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups sifted flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Heat oil to 360-370 F
  2. Add sugar to melted shortning and beat thoroughly
  3. Beat eggs and add to sugar
  4. Whisk dry ingredients together
  5. Alternately add portions of dry ingredients then milk until done
  6. Roll 1/4 inch thick on floured surface. Refrigerate if too soft
  7. Cut with doughnut cutter
  8. Fry a few at a time in deep fat (360 F), turning once
  9. Drain and frost.

Notes

I plan on trying this in air fryer.
