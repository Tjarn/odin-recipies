Quick Coffee Cake

Description

Makes one 9 inch x 9 inch coffeecake

This is a good, basic coffee cake -- it's easy to modtfy, just add in a cup or so of your flavoring. I like frozen blueberries, for example.

Ingredients

Basic Cake

  • 1 1/2 cups sifted flour -- 195 grams grain
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted shortning

Crumb Topping

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup sifted flour -- 32 1/2 grams grain
  • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. grind 227.5 grams of grain.
    32.5 grams go to topping
    195 grams go to the cake itself -- I like rye and wheat mixed and ground together.
  2. Prepare Crumb topping by creaming 2 tablespoons each of butter and sugar, then mixing in the flour, crumbs, and cinnamon. It should be a coarse meal texture.
  3. Set topping aside and preheat oven to 425 F
  4. Whisk dry ingredients together.
  5. Beat egg and add milk and melted shortning
  6. Gently stir liquid into dry ingredients, stirring only enough to dampen flour
  7. Pour into greased 9 x 9 inch pan
  8. Sprinkle with crumb topping
  9. Bake at 425 F for 25 minutes
