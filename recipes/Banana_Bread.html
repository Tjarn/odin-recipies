Banana Bread

Description

Makes 1 loaf, approx 1 lb.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shortning
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mashed bananas
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 cups SIFTED flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped nut meats

Steps

  1. Cream shortening and sugar together
  2. Beat eggs until light and add to s&s
  3. Mix lemon juice with banana pulp and add
  4. Whisk dry ingredients together and add
  5. Mix lightly and turn into greased loaf pan
  6. Bake ~ 75 minutes @ 375 F

Notes

Be sure to keep flour fluffed up when measuring. I used 1/2 cup rye grain and 1/2 cup wheatberries ground together and had a lot left over. It made a delicious loaf.
